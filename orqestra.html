Event orchestration infrastructure

Orchestrate your platform.
Not just your services.

A modular infrastructure suite for event-driven, multi-tenant systems. Built for platform teams that need reliability, control, and scale.

The problem

Modern systems are fragmented.

As platforms grow, the boundaries between business logic and infrastructure blur. Teams ship faster, but the system gets harder to reason about.

  • Notification and webhook code copy-pasted across every service
  • Business logic married to delivery channels — changing one breaks the other
  • No single place to trace what was sent, why, or whether it was delivered
  • Quotas and per-tenant config that scale exponentially with each service
The approach

One orchestration layer. Multiple capabilities.

Orqestra sits between your services and your infrastructure, transforming events into coordinated system behavior.

Not a collection of tools — a unified layer where multiple system capabilities are coordinated through events. Decoupled by design, multi-tenant by default.

The suite

Built independently. Designed to work together.

Each product stands on its own. Together, they form a coherent platform plane.

Point solutions solve one channel of pain. Orqestra solves the orchestration problem underneath — so the next capability is a config, not a rewrite.

Available now

Orqestra Notifications.

A multi-tenant, event-driven notification engine with reliable delivery, intelligent routing, and full observability.

  • Kafka-backed event pipeline
  • Multi-channel — Email, SMS, Push, WebSocket, Webhook
  • DLQ + retry handling with idempotency
  • Template management via MJML
  • Provider fallback system
